Sarawak Government Commitment

Sarawak Chief Minister Datuk Patinggi Abang Johari Openg announced that forest restoration is one of the main agenda of the state government for years to come.

The sole purpose is to enrich the logged-over areas, especially permanent forest estates with high value timber species. Forest restoration is crucial as it keeps the forest alive and ensuring the overall ecosystem can carry its natural functions. Furthermore, it is to ensure that our forest can have sufficient and sustainable timber production.

Our Goals

1. Regain ecological functionality of forest in Sarawak

2. Enhance human well-being and livelihood of forest-dependent communities

3. Re-establish the structure, productivity, and species diversity of forest

4. Ensure sufficient future timber stocking for sustainable forest management
